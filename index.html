The Manifesto
Brand Manifesto
— Written in full
001
Most clothing asks nothing of you.
We ask everything.
There is a kind of dressing that is purely functional. We believe a garment should demand awareness, intention, and a point of view.
002
We build from the inside out.
Every piece starts as a structural problem. Form follows intention, and every detail earns its place or doesn't exist at all.
